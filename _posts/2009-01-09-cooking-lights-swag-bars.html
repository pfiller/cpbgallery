I noticed a CPB recipe in the latest issue of Cooking Light that looks tasty and wanted to share. This bar is a light recreation of a CPB bar made by the chef at The Swag Country Inn in Waynesville, North Carolina. It's packed with peanut butter, lightly salted dry-roasted peanuts, whole-grain flaked cereal (like Total) and drizzled with dark chocolate. Can't hop the train to Waynesville to stake out the kitchen where these bars are made? No problem - get the lightened copycat recipe at Cooking Light.
